## 🔄 How It Works

### For Customers
1. **Search**: Find the construction materials or services you need.
2. **Order**: Choose between Cash on Delivery (COD) or your "Pay Later" credit limit.
3. **Bill Type**: Select "GST Bill" (for ITC claims) or "Simple Bill" (for retail/personal use) at checkout. The GST option is only shown if the vendor is GST-registered.
4. **Negotiate**: For large quantities, send a "Bulk Request" message to the vendor.
5. **Receive**: Track your delivery in real-time and confirm receipt.
6. **Download Bill**: Once the vendor uploads the bill, download it directly from "My Orders."
7. **Rate**: Leave a review to maintain high standards on the platform.

### For Vendors
1. **List**: Upload your catalogue with high-quality images and accurate specifications.
2. **Update**: Follow the daily price update schedule to keep your listings competitive.
3. **Fulfill**: Manage orders through your dashboard and update statuses promptly.
4. **Upload Bill**: After delivery, upload the official bill (GST invoice or simple receipt) from the Orders dashboard. Each order card shows the customer's requested bill type as a badge.
5. **Withdraw**: Request payouts from your wallet as orders are completed and audited.
6. **Maintain**: Pay weekly commission invoices on time to avoid "Strikes" and account blocks.

## 🧾 GST Billing System

ConEco supports flexible billing to meet the needs of both retail and commercial buyers.

### Bill Types

| Bill Type | Purpose | Who Uses It |
|---|---|---|
| **GST Bill** | Formal Tax Invoice with vendor's GSTIN — eligible for Input Tax Credit (ITC) claims | Businesses buying for commercial use |
| **Non-GST / Simple Bill** | Standard receipt without formal tax invoice | Individuals or businesses that don't need ITC |

### Key Rules
- The **"GST Bill"** option is only available if the vendor in the cart has a registered GST number in their profile. If not, the option is grayed out automatically.
- The **total order amount is the same** regardless of bill type. GST on the product is always included in the price as required by law. The bill type only controls the format of the invoice document.
- After delivery, vendors upload the bill document from their dashboard. Customers can then download it from "My Orders" or "My Booked Services."

## 📲 Daily Price Update Protocol (WhatsApp)

To maintain the accuracy of the platform, Vendors and Admins work together every morning to synchronize prices.

### ⏰ The 9-to-10 Window
- **9:00 AM**: Vendors send their updated price lists to the Admin via WhatsApp.
- **10:00 AM**: Admin completes the bulk updates on the platform, ensuring the site is ready for the day's business.

> 
> **Vendor Name**: [Your Name/Company Name as per Platform]  
> **Date**: [Today's Date]
> 
> **Product Updates**:
> 1. [Product Name A] — ₹[New Price]
> 2. [Product Name B] — ₹[New Price]
> 3. [Product Name C] — ₹[New Price]
>
