# ConEco — Platform Overview & Guidelines

Welcome to **ConEco**, a professional B2B marketplace designed to bridge the gap between Vendors and Customers with transparency, efficiency, and trust. This document outlines everything you need to know about how the platform works and what it offers.

---

## 🚀 The ConEco Mission
ConEco is more than just an app; it is a business ecosystem. We simplify bulk procurement, facilitate credit-based trading, and ensure high-quality standards through automated QC and real-time tracking.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🛍️ For Customers (Buyers)
- **Bulk Price Negotiation**: Don't settle for retail prices. Request custom quotes for large orders directly from the vendor.
- **Pay Later (Credit System)**: Boost your cash flow with 7-day or 14-day credit limits assigned by the Admin.
- **Real-time Order Tracking**: Receive instant Web Push and Email notifications from "Order Placed" to "Delivered."
- **PWA Experience**: Install ConEco on your phone's home screen for a fast, app-like experience.

### 🏪 For Vendors (Sellers)
- **Comprehensive Dashboard**: Track your Gross Sales, Platform Commissions, and Net Earnings in real-time.
- **Vendor Wallet**: Automated tracking of earnings with a simple "Withdrawal Request" system for bank payouts.
- **QC Reliability Score**: High-performing vendors receive better visibility through our Quality Control scoring system.
- **Weekly Billing**: Automated invoices generated every Monday for easy settlement of platform commissions.

## 🔄 How It Works

### For Customers
1. **Search**: Find the construction materials or services you need.
2. **Order**: Choose between Cash on Delivery (COD) or your "Pay Later" credit limit.
3. **Negotiate**: For large quantities, send a "Bulk Request" message to the vendor.
4. **Receive**: Track your delivery in real-time and confirm receipt.
5. **Rate**: Leave a review to maintain high standards on the platform.

### For Vendors
1. **List**: Upload your catalogue with high-quality images and accurate specifications.
2. **Update**: Follow the daily price update schedule to keep your listings competitive.
3. **Fulfill**: Manage orders through your dashboard and update statuses promptly.
4. **Withdraw**: Request payouts from your wallet as orders are completed and audited.
5. **Maintain**: Pay weekly commission invoices on time to avoid "Strikes" and account blocks.

## 📲 Daily Price Update Protocol (WhatsApp)

To maintain the accuracy of the platform, Vendors and Admins work together every morning to synchronize prices.

### ⏰ The 9-to-10 Window
- **9:00 AM**: Vendors send their updated price lists to the Admin via WhatsApp.
- **10:00 AM**: Admin completes the bulk updates on the platform, ensuring the site is ready for the day's business.

### 📝 WhatsApp Message Template
Vendors should use the following format for daily updates:

> 📌 **DAILY PRICE UPDATE — CONECO**
> 
> **Vendor Name**: [Your Name/Company Name as per Platform]
> **Date**: [Today's Date]
> 
> **Product Updates**:
> 1. [Product Name A] — ₹[New Price]
> 2. [Product Name B] — ₹[New Price]
> 3. [Product Name C] — ₹[New Price]
> 
> *Note: Please mention if any product is currently 'Out of Stock' or if there are new arrivals.*
