## ⚠️ Important Note Before Starting

The rewards in the Con-Eco Referral Program are:
- **Customers** → earn **Wallet Credits** (used for purchases on Con-Eco)
- **Vendors** → earn **Listing Credits** (used for featured listings on Con-Eco)
- **New joiners** → get a **Welcome Bonus Credit** on their first order/listing

> ❌ These are NOT real cash payouts.  
> ✅ Always say "wallet credit", "listing credit", or "platform credit" in all videos.

## 3. Step-by-Step Workflow

### Using InVideo AI (For V1 and V3)

```
Step 1 → Open https://invideo.ai → Sign up free
Step 2 → Click "AI Video" → Select "Text to Video"
Step 3 → Paste the script prompt from Section 4 or 6 below
Step 4 → Choose Style: "Corporate" or "Modern Business"
Step 5 → Choose Voice: Indian English Male or Female
Step 6 → Click Generate → Wait 2–3 minutes
Step 7 → Review the draft video
Step 8 → Replace stock clips with your own Con-Eco screenshots/logo
Step 9 → Adjust subtitle style to match brand colors
Step 10 → Export as MP4 (720p for WhatsApp, 1080p for Instagram/LinkedIn)
```

### Using Canva (For V2)

```
Step 1 → Open https://canva.com → Create New → Video → 1080x1920 (Reels)
Step 2 → Search template: "App Promo" or "Badge Award"
Step 3 → Build 5 slides (see Section 5 for slide content)
Step 4 → Use Canva "Animate" feature: fade-in, slide-up for each badge
Step 5 → Add free background music from Canva library
Step 6 → Use "Present and Record" → export as MP4
```

## 4. Video 1 — Prompt & Script

**Title:** "Refer. Join. Get Rewarded."  
**Tool:** InVideo AI  
**Audience:** Existing Customers  
**Duration:** ~40 seconds

---

### 🤖 Paste This Prompt into InVideo AI

```
Create a 40-second promotional video for a B2B marketplace called "Con-Eco" 
(short for Construction-Ecosystem). This video is about their new Referral Program 
for Customers.

Style: Clean, modern corporate. Warm and trustworthy. Indian business visuals.
Voiceover: Indian English, friendly and professional tone.
Music: Soft upbeat corporate background music.

Script to follow exactly:

[Scene 1 - 0 to 8 seconds]
Screen text: "You love using Con-Eco. Now share it — and get rewarded."
Visual: A person browsing a marketplace on laptop/phone, smiling.

[Scene 2 - 8 to 18 seconds]
Screen text: "Here's how the Referral Program works:"
Visual: Step-by-step graphic
Step 1: You get your unique referral code from your Con-Eco dashboard.
Step 2: Share it with a friend or business contact.
Step 3: They register using your code.
Step 4: They place their first order — you both earn platform credits!

[Scene 3 - 18 to 30 seconds]
Screen text: "The more you refer, the bigger your rewards:"
Visual: Progress bar / milestone badges appearing one by one.
→ 1 referral = Wallet Credit (Bronze Milestone)
→ 5 referrals = More Wallet Credit + Connector Badge (Silver)
→ 10 referrals = Priority Support + Gold Ambassador status
→ 25 referrals = Platinum Elite perks

Note: Say "wallet credit" — these are platform credits used on Con-Eco.
Do NOT say "cash" or "money".

[Scene 4 - 30 to 38 seconds]
Screen text: "Your friend also wins! New joiners get a Welcome Credit on their first order."
Visual: Two people high-fiving or shaking hands.

[Scene 5 - 38 to 45 seconds]
Screen text: "Log in to Con-Eco → Go to Refer & Earn → Copy your code → Start sharing!"
Visual: App/website on screen. Con-Eco logo bottom right.
End card: "con-eco.in"

Subtitles: Yes, auto-generate. White bold text with dark background for readability.
```
